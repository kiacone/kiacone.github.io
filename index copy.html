Kristin Iacone

Designer & Developer

Hi, I’m Kristin. I live in Lakeside with my boyfriend Scott, two cats Zoe and Panda and Iguana Nushi – who lives in one of our closets. In my spare time, I think of myself as a pseudo decorator and I love dreaming of all the things I’ll do to my house one day. When not working, you can find me reading for my book club, traveling and singing (badly) to my cats. I love to impress people with my unnatural ability to quote movies and I’m a metal kid at heart but find with age that my range of music has expanded greatly.

I’m currently a Design Lead on the Digital Messaging Design, Development and Innovation team at Capital One. I fell into email design and development and love how niche the channel can be. I enjoy solving problems and making the seemingly impossible possible - which happens quite a bit in the world of email. My happy place is when I can design something that solves a need for the user.
